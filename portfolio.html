My Personal Journal

This interactive daily journal allows you to effortlessly manage tasks, set priorities, and take notes. You can easily add new tasks, mark them as completed by moving them to the 'Finished Tasks' section, delete them, or promote them to priorities when needed. For priorities, you can add, remove, and reorder them using a simple drag-and-drop interface. Additionally, you can select your mood for the day and the current day of the week with a single click, adding a personal touch to your journaling experience. The entire app is styled with a fun Halloween theme to give it a unique and festive flair.
